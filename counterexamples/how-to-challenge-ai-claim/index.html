Format

A useful challenge is specific.

Name the public claim, quote or link the relevant page, state the evidence gap, and identify the failure class: stronger baseline, reproduction failure, leakage, overbroad boundary, stale evidence, or authority leak.

The purpose is not to win an argument. The purpose is to make the public record narrower, stronger, or visibly unresolved.

Repair

A valid challenge should change the record.

If the challenge is valid, the page should update status, boundary, evidence route, or next review. If it is invalid, the rejection reason should still be explainable.

That is the difference between community criticism and evidence production.

Boundary

What this page does not prove.

  • Do not request private credentials, customer data, private logs, or restricted submission material.
  • Do not treat harassment, private-system probing, or credential requests as public validation.
  • A counterexample should attack a public claim through public evidence routes.
FAQ

What is the minimum useful challenge?

A claim URL, evidence gap, failure class, and requested correction.

FAQ

Can I challenge private runtime claims?

Only public claims should be challenged through public routes; private material is not required for public boundary review.

FAQ

What happens after a valid challenge?

The claim should be narrowed, marked pending, corrected, or routed to a next review.
