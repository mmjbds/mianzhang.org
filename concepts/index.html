Concept Index

Stable names for public evidence.

Each term has a fixed definition, evidence scope, DOI/GitHub/HF links, counterexample route, and boundary statement.

Concept

No-Proof No-Action Gate

No-Proof No-Action Gate means that a high-risk AI system must enter a visible no-action state when required proof, authority, or scope is missing.

Concept

Self-Certification Grounding Check

Self-Certification Grounding Check means that a system's self-report must be grounded in public records, failure states, unresolved states, expiry conditions, and external constraints.

Open concept entry

Concept

Proof-Carrying Action

Proof-Carrying Action means that an action claim must carry public proof fields, authority scope, refusal records, evidence boundaries, and counterexample routes before it can be trusted.

Open concept entry

Concept

WisdomBench

WisdomBench is a public benchmark line for evaluating longitudinal failure learning, recovery, grader drift, and wisdom-oriented behavior under bounded evidence.

Open concept entry

Concept

Robot/Vision Downgrade Rule

Robot/Vision Downgrade Rule means that uncertain perception evidence must route to review, stop, or wording that exactly matches the validated perception envelope; it must not become deployment language.

Open concept entry

Concept

The Grounding Canon

The Grounding Canon is the P46 public line for reporting claim-to-evidence ladders across theory, constructed witnesses, shadow evidence, and human-reviewed records.
