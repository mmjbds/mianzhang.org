AI time savings can rebound into added work before anyone notices.

Artifact

Efficiency claims need a ledger, not a slogan.

AI can reduce one task while raising the floor for everything around it. The review object asks what was saved, what was added, who recovered time in practice, and who captured the benefit.

Ledger

A saved hour is not a saved life.

If the output floor shifts upward, the old promise of productivity can become a disguised obligation. The ledger keeps the accounting visible so the system does not turn a tool gain into a management tax.

Review

If recovery time does not appear, the gain is incomplete.

A useful challenge should identify a missing field, an unsupported causal link, a stale evidence timestamp, or a condition under which the proposed control fails. If the benefit lands somewhere other than the user, the ledger has to say that too.
