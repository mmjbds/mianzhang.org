Positioning

Reliable intelligence after failure.

Wisdom Science studies whether AI systems improve after experience, failure, feedback, perturbation, and recovery, rather than only measuring first-attempt capability.

The Chinese entry explains the same evidence discipline for readers who want the project in Chinese: reliable action after failure, proof-carrying records, public boundaries, and reproducible challenges.
