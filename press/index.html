Positioning

Reliable intelligence after failure.

Wisdom Science studies whether AI systems improve after experience, failure, feedback, perturbation, and recovery, rather than only measuring first-attempt capability.

中文定位：参数变大不是 AI 落地的终点。真正缺的是可靠行动：证据、记忆、失败恢复、工作流、分寸感、治理和复盘。

Boundary

Claim Discipline

Do not frame the work as universal proof, detector SOTA, real-robot deployment, financial advice, medical validity, or autonomous enforcement.

Tone

Public Voice

Calm, rigorous, evidence-gated, and artistic. Strong claims should be paired with artifacts, limits, and next validation steps.

Visual Assets

Use restrained, high-trust visuals.

The public visual language is black, warm ivory, gold, and restrained red. Prefer evidence gates, closed loops, body-like architectures, and research diagrams. Avoid weapon imagery, hype graphics, clutter, and tiny text.
